Our Story

Piacere Mio means “my pleasure.”

This little bakery dream began with a love of Italian food traditions, travel memories, and the simple comfort found in village cafés throughout Italy.

For now, Piacere Mio is a place to explore recipes, learn, bake, and share the foods that bring people together.
